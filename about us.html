About Us

         
Welcome to E-store, your number one source for grocery. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of groceries, with a focus on three characteristics, ie: dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Found in 2019 by Aakash, Lokesh and Manthan, E-store has come a long way from its beginnings in a class room at Lovely Professional University, Phagwara, Punjab, India. When we first started out, our passion for providing a store on the go, drove us to think, and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over Punjab, Himachal Pradesh and Chandigarh, and are thrilled to be a part of thefair trade wing of the e-commerce industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
